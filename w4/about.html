Why am I taking this course?

I’m really excited to learn coding because I want to be able to create cute, playful designs and bring my ideas to life on the web. Being in this CIT85 class, learning HTML and CSS, feels like the perfect start to becoming a “cool coder” who can make things that not only look adorable but work beautifully too. Learning these skills opens up so many creative doors, letting me build unique projects from scratch and personalize them. Plus, each project helps me get a stronger foundation in web design and development, which will be so valuable as I keep growing in this field. I love the idea of coding my own colorful, interactive sites that can inspire others and make them smile. This class also gives me a chance to learn alongside others who are as passionate as I am. Each new skill I gain brings me closer to my dream of making a creative impact online!
