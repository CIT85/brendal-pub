MIKA
Mika is a level 10 bubbly and outgoing high school girl with vibrant pink hair styled in playful twin tails, always adorned with cute hair clips. She’s known for her cheerful personality, infectious laugh, and a love for all things sweet—especially strawberry parfaits. Despite her carefree attitude, Mika is surprisingly thoughtful, often leaving little notes of encouragement for her friends. She’s a member of the school’s fashion club, always rocking the latest trends with her own unique twist.

What I input into ChatGPT to get this image result = "can you show me a picture with a cute pink anime girl with school outfit with video game health stats in a corner".

⋆ ★ lvl 25 ★ ⋆

SAYAKA
Sayaka is a stylish and energetic level 25 college gyaru with long, wavy blonde hair and a playful smile that lights up any room. She’s always dressed in the latest fashion, rocking pastel nails, flashy accessories, and a touch of pink makeup. Sayaka is the life of the party, but beneath her bubbly and teasing nature, she has a surprisingly kind and supportive heart. She loves hanging out at cafés, taking selfies, and giving her friends makeovers, always making sure everyone around her feels confident. Despite her carefree attitude, she secretly worries about the future and finding someone who sees her for more than just her looks.

What I input into ChatGPT to get this image result = "can you make an image of an anime typical gyaru girl with blonde hair pink make up on her phone with video game stats in the corner".

⋆ ★ lvl 75 ★ ⋆

YUYA
Yuya is a level 75 quiet yet captivating college student with striking red-and-black hair, dark eyeliner, and a love for all things alternative. Often seen wearing oversized hoodies, ripped tights, and chunky boots, she has an effortlessly cool vibe that keeps people intrigued. Though she comes off as distant at first, Yuya has a deep love for music, often spending hours in record stores or playing her guitar in the park. She has a soft spot for late-night café hangs, rainy days, and deep conversations about life and dreams. Beneath her tough, independent exterior, she secretly craves connection but struggles to let people in.
